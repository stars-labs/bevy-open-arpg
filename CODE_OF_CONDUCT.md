# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, nationality, race, religion, sex, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, and sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Respectful, inclusive communication.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, and discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or stalking.
- Publishing private information without consent.

Project maintainer contact: open issues and PRs in a respectful tone, and we will address
policy violations promptly.
